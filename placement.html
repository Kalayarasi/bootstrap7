About Placement Cell

The Placement Cell was established in 1998. The cell arranges campus interviews with organizations for placement of final year students, and arranges in-industry training for students. Campus placement training (HR, Technical, GD, Written Test) is given in the pre-final year for all eligible candidates. Kongu ENgineering College has recently signed an MoU with CoreEL Technologies that helps conduct various training activities
